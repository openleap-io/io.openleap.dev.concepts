# Glossary Term: Storage Unit

> **Template:** [Glossary Term](../../../../concepts/artifacts/product/glossary-term.md)

---

## Fields

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| id | storage-unit |
| term | Storage Unit |
| definition | A trackable quantity of a single material stored in a specific bin location. A storage unit ties together a material, a quantity, a lot/batch number, and a bin location. Storage units are created during put-away and consumed during picking. |
| aliases | SU, Lagereinheit |
| functionalAreaRef | storage |

---

## Examples

- "Storage unit SU-20260329-001: 200 pcs of Steel bolt M8x50 in Bin A-03-2-1, Lot L-2026-0100."
- "This bin has 3 storage units — two different materials and one material split across two lots."
