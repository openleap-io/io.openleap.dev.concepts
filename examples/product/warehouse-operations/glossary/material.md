# Glossary Term: Material

> **Template:** [Glossary Term](../../../../artifacts/product/glossary-term.md)

---

## Fields

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| id | material |
| term | Material |
| definition | A physical product, raw material, or component managed in the warehouse. Each material has a unique SKU, a base unit of measure, and attributes governing storage and handling (e.g., regulated status, storage type). Material master data is owned by PPS-PD and consumed read-only by warehouse operations. |
| aliases | Item, Part, SKU |
| functionalAreaRef | storage |

---

## Examples

- "Steel bolt M8x50" — a standard material stored in bins, measured in pcs.
- "Isopropanol 99%" — a regulated material requiring hazmat storage and quality check on receipt.
