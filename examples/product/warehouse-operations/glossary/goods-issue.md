# Glossary Term: Goods Issue

> **Template:** [Glossary Term](../../../../concepts/artifacts/product/glossary-term.md)

---

## Fields

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| id | goods-issue |
| term | Goods Issue |
| definition | The formal act of dispatching materials from the warehouse to an external recipient (typically a customer via a logistics partner). A goods issue decrements inventory, creates a shipment record, and triggers notification to the logistics partner. |
| aliases | GI, Outbound Delivery, Warenausgang |
| functionalAreaRef | outbound |

---

## Examples

- "We completed 12 goods issues today — all dispatched via DHL."
- "The goods issue for delivery order DO-2026-0500 is waiting for the carrier to arrive."

## Not to Confuse With

| Term Ref | Explanation |
|----------|-------------|
| goods-receipt | Goods Issue is outbound (materials leaving the warehouse), Goods Receipt is inbound (materials arriving). They are opposite operations. |
