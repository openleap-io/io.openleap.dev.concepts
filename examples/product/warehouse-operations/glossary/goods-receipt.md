# Glossary Term: Goods Receipt

> **Template:** [Glossary Term](../../../../artifacts/product/glossary-term.md)

---

## Fields

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| id | goods-receipt |
| term | Goods Receipt |
| definition | The formal act of receiving materials from a supplier into the warehouse, including verification against a purchase order, optional quality check, quantity confirmation, and posting to inventory. A goods receipt transitions materials from "in transit" to "in warehouse." |
| aliases | GR, Incoming Delivery, Wareneingang |
| functionalAreaRef | inbound |

---

## Examples

- "We posted 3 goods receipts this morning — two from Supplier A and one from Supplier B."
- "The goods receipt for PO-2026-0412 is still pending because the quality check hasn't been completed."

## Not to Confuse With

| Term Ref | Explanation |
|----------|-------------|
| goods-issue | Goods Receipt is inbound (materials coming in), Goods Issue is outbound (materials going out). They are opposite operations. |
