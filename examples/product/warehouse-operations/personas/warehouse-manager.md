## Pain Points

- No real-time visibility into bin utilization or stock levels
- Excel-based bin management is fragile and often outdated
- Cannot identify bottlenecks until end-of-week reports
- High-value goods receipt approval requires physical presence at the dock

---

## Workarounds

- Manually consolidates data from paper forms into Excel every evening
- Walks the floor to visually estimate utilization
- Uses WhatsApp group to coordinate with workers for urgent picks
- Prints daily summary reports from legacy ERP for morning briefing
