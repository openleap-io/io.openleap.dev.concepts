## Pain Points

- Matching paper delivery notes to purchase orders in the ERP is slow (requires switching between screens)
- Quality check status is communicated verbally, leading to missed checks on regulated materials
- Discrepancies between expected and actual quantities require manual escalation with no audit trail
- Goods receipt posting is delayed because data must be re-entered from paper forms

---

## Workarounds

- Prints purchase orders in advance and clips them to a clipboard for dock use
- Maintains a personal checklist of regulated materials that need quality checks
- Takes photos of delivery notes with personal phone as backup documentation
- Flags discrepancies with sticky notes on the paper form for later resolution
