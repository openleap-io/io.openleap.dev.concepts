## Pain Points

- Paper-based pick lists are hard to read and get damaged
- Walking back and forth because bin locations are unclear or wrong
- Manual data entry into ERP at end of shift is tedious and error-prone
- No real-time feedback on whether a pick or put-away was recorded correctly

---

## Workarounds

- Keeps personal notes on frequently used bin locations
- Writes quantities on hand with marker when paper list gets wet
- Calls manager on radio when unsure about bin assignment
- Batches ERP data entry to end of day, leading to stale inventory data
